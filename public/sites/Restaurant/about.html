About Us

This site isn't really anything special, I know. It was actually one of the first few websites I built, so I like to leave it up for nostalgic purposes. It's just something I whipped up in part of an afternoon as an example for a prospective client of what a static site for their restaurant might look like. It was something I had heard about through a friend, and of course I never heard back on it. However, I still learned a lot about the process of working with clients, gathering and clarifying user requirements, and managing time when I'm dictating the entire process among other things. It's no Notre-Dame, but I feel that it's still a relevant part of my journey.
